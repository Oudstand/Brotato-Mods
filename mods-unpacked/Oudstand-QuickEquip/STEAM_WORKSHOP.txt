[h1]QuickEquip - Instant Equipment Management[/h1]

Quickly equip items and weapons during runs! QuickEquip lets you add or remove equipment anytime - perfect for testing builds, experimenting with loadouts, or setting up challenge runs.

[h2]Features[/h2]

[list]
[*][b]Instant Equipment[/b] - Add or remove weapons and items during active runs
[*][b]Live Updates[/b] - All changes apply immediately without restarting
[*][b]Smart Item Selection[/b] - Deduplicated lists with item icons and separate tier dropdowns
[*][b]Dynamic Tier Selection[/b] - Only shows available tiers for each item
[*][b]Full Control[/b] - Set quantity and mark items as cursed
[*][b]Multilingual[/b] - Built-in English and German translations
[*][b]Clean UI[/b] - Integrated with ModOptions for consistent interface
[/list]

[h2]Usage[/h2]

[b]1. Open Options[/b] (ESC) during a run or in the main menu
[b]2. Go to Mods → Quick Equip[/b]
[b]3. Configure your equipment:[/b]

[h3]Adding Weapons/Items[/h3]
[list]
[*]Click [b]+ Add Weapon[/b] or [b]+ Add Item[/b]
[*]Select from dropdown with icons
[*]Choose tier (I, II, III, IV) - only available tiers shown
[*]Set quantity
[*]Optionally mark as cursed
[*]Changes apply immediately if you're in a run!
[/list]

[h3]Removing Equipment[/h3]
[list]
[*]Click the [b]X[/b] button next to any item/weapon
[*]Equipment removed from inventory instantly
[/list]

[h2]Smart Features[/h2]

[list]
[*]Items with minimum tier requirements (e.g., Anchor requires Tier II, Dex Sweater requires Tier IV) automatically filter unavailable tiers
[*]If an item only exists in one tier, the tier dropdown is hidden
[*]Equipment list is deduplicated - each item appears once with separate tier selection
[*]Icons make finding items quick and easy
[/list]

[h2]Use Cases[/h2]

[list]
[*][b]Build Testing[/b] - Quickly test weapon and item combinations
[*][b]Challenge Runs[/b] - Start with specific equipment for custom challenges
[*][b]Learning[/b] - Experiment with items you haven't unlocked yet
[*][b]Speed Testing[/b] - Skip shopping and jump straight to testing
[*][b]Content Creation[/b] - Set up specific builds for videos or screenshots
[/list]
